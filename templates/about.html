Data Sources

a. College Scorecard (dataset published by the USA Department of Education).
This dataset contains over 1800 features about academic, admissions, completion, student body, etc., for over 7500 college institutions in the United States. Furthermore, the dataset provides information about each academic year, starting in 1996-1997 and ending in 2015-2016.

b. Twitter API.
I intend to collect a dataset containing tweets with location and geographycal information such that I can calculate the amount of social network usage per capita segmented by location (statewide) and time (year wide).

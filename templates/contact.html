About me:

I am an experimental physicist with over 8 years of experience in a wide range of applied physics techniques. During my doctoral studies, I worked on several experiments to demonstrate efficient and robust quantum control and quantum measurement schemes using ultracold neutral atoms.

Currently, I work alongside a group of graduate students building an experiment to produce quantum degenerate gases of sodium and erbium. In this experiment we are planning to investigate superfluidity effects, as well as, physics of gases with dipolar interactions.

Expertise: Project Management, Physics Engineering, Research & Development, Data Analysis, Physical Optics, Laser Systems, Electronics, Signal Processing, Matlab Programming.

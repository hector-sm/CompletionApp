What is the objective of #Completion?

In this project, my goal is to evaluate the relationship between social networks usage and students academic performance. In particular, I would like to find out if there exist a positive or negative correlation between social networks usage and college completion rates for a given academic year and state in the United States.

What is the project Motivation?

Over the past decade, daily use of social networks has become a common part of our lifestyle. Just in the United States and Canada the number of Facebook users has increased from 130 to 240 million from 2010 to 2017. Twitter shows a similar trend, going from 10 to 70 million in the same period. This quick rise in popularity, can partially be explained by the heavy usage of social network sites among college students.
This unprecedented scenario has triggered global efforts to investigate the consequences of social networking sites. Among these questions is the one regarding the effects of Facebook and Twitter on academic performance.
